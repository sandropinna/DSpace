HOW TO SUBMIT NEW ITEMS

(articles, preprints, theses ...)


Only employees and students of the SISSA may submit material in SDL. Before starting, make login with SISSA network username and password, then click the "Submissions" link in the menu section headed My Account, and follow the step-by-step submission procedure.

Select Collection

Move your mouse to the collection into which you wish to add your item and click.

Click on the "Next" button to proceed.

Archive identifier

If the item was submitted to arXiv.org or PubMed repository, enter the identifier to harvest the data (e.g. arXiv:1102.4200v1, arXiv:0911.3757, arXiv:hep-lat/0701012v2, PMID:20637259)

The main data of your paper will be automatically retrieved and entered in this record.

Click on the "Next" button to proceed, or "Save & Exit" button to stop and save or cancel your submission.
